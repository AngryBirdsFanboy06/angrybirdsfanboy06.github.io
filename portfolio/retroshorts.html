RetroShorts YouTube channel

Basic Information

RetroShorts is a YouTube channel dedicated to making RetroStudio short animations based on existing media (such as Vine, TikTok and more), as well as live-streaming RetroStudio and more.

The channel was created on September 2nd, 2021 and the first short was uploaded a few days later.
As of me writing this (April 21st, 2026), the channel has 4k+ subscribers, as well as almost 1 million views in total.

The videos and shorts heavily utilise characters, props and backgrounds from RetroStudio, the animating is made using Key Framing via Adobe Premiere Pro. The videos and shorts also reuse infamous audios from known places such as the defunct platform "Vine" for example.

The channel was originally planned to be a community focused first, with the plan of giving people the ability to make their own RetroStudio animations, but that got scrapped due to burn out.

The end of RetroShorts

On September 12th, 2026, after the 5th anniversary of RetroShorts. The channel will post a final RetroShorts YouTube short before going inactive.

The reason why I'm ending RetroShorts is for couple of things.
The main reasons are as following: I want to move on from making Roblox stuff, I don't want to continue making stuff on a channel that I don't feel okay with monitizing due to me using actual people in the animations and just using the Retro Dev community to make these stuff and more.

Thank you for supporting me on this journey.
