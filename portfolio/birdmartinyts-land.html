Basic Information

birdmartinyt's Land is a showcase game that I made during my teens.

Before Version 1.3 (Around 2021), the game looked barebones, as I was mainly learning how to use Roblox Studio at the time.

Around Feburary 2023, I published a huge makeover update, where I improved the graphics, extended the water and more.

Not much to say, other than a ex-YouTuber with under 100k subs played it before and was featured in their video.

Will probably open source this game someday via Github. :P
