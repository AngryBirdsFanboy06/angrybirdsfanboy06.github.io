Basic Information

Blox Hunt is a RetroStudio game created by Rixxles, which is a close remake of the 2014 'Blox Hunt' made by Aqualotl.

The game is about players who can hide as various objects around the map to outsmart the seekers. It was featured in the official RetroStudio Giftsplosion 2024 event.
