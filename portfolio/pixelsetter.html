Basic Information

Pixel Setter was a website, where you can draw on a blank canvas with other people, similar to r/place. It was created and managed by chocolateimage.

In the website, there were multiple canvas to draw on, as well as putting image to the canvas, only locally at the users end to trace. It was also live-streamed on chocolateimage's youtube channel.

My Involvement

Moderation, participating as well as posting to the Pixel Setter Playlook clan.
