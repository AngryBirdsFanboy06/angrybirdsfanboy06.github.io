RetroStudio

Basic Information

RetroStudio is a game platform/onsite Roblox revival within Roblox, where users can create games that can simulate any time period between early 2009 to mid 2015.

The game itself is based on RetroStudio's precursor, RETROBLOX but with their own in-game avatar system, Studio mode, Contests and more.

My Involvement

Play-tested RetroStudio events for the event team (Such as the Summer Games Event) from April 2024 to February 2025, as well as making few custom content that are in the catalog.

Around February 2025, I got fired from the tester team, due to a miscommunication and disagreement with each other.
