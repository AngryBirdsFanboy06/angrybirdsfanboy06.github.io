Basic Information

Retro Dev Out Of Context or RetroDevOOC for short was a Out of Context Twitter account formally ran by Patomario, ThomasLuigi07 and myself.

The account posted out of context screenshots such as from RetroStudio, Retro Dev Discord server or anything Retro Dev Related.

The account got deactivated due to personal reasons on August 31st, 2025.

On October 21st, 2024, Notire made a RetrodevOOC Revival account on Bluesky. Which funny enough, I also contribue to, despite me not planning to do it after I left Twitter for good.

My Involvement

Posting out of context screenshots and accepting submissions until on December 30th, 2023.
