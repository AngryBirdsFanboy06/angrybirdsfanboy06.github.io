Basic Information

AI Simpsons was an AI-generated livestream show inspired by ai_sponge and the original Simpsons TV show where you can create topics for the characters to discuss, including crazy effects and features. The dialogue and voices are AI generated, while the source code is human written.

While AI Human Simulation on the other hand, was a different project, where you control a human and decide on what it should do.
