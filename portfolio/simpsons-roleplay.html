Basic Information

The Simpsons Roleplay is a Roblox Roleplay game, based on both The Simpsons Hit & Run and originally AI Simpsons by chocolateimage (Back when AI wasnt taken seriously and wasnt problematic as it is now).
In this game, you can morph yourself as Simpsons characters and roleplay as them, along with exploring the map and finding collectables, unlock more skins and etc.

On May 18th, 2026. The game got discontinued due to Roblox making updates that hurt the game in the long run, such as the Age Group Chat update and the nail in the coffin, the new game publishing requirements.
and because we have no intrest in giving our personal information such as ID cards, because of how poorly Roblox and Persona manage it.

My Involvement

Designed most of the badges, gamepasses and the thumbnails for the game, as well as working on few original morphs, map improvements and other minor changes.
I especially liked the redesigning of the secret room, where I fixed an morph as well as adding a new one.
