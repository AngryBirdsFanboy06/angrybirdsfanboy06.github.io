Basic Information

Finn's Obby World is a Roblox obby game created by TheFinnishOne, the game has bunch of random and bite-sized obbies to complete.

The game is officially in hiatus, due to Roblox forcing everyone to verify their ages in order to chat and other questionable updates. But in my opinion, I don't see this game not getting cancelled because of the way Roblox is going.
