Basic Information

Roblox Bloopers is a "Roblox Animated Comedy Series" that's heavily inspired by older SMG4's "Super Mario 64 Bloopers" with a Roblox coat of paint.

The series involves around Newbie and his group of buddies doing wacky and silly adventures with a little bit of edgy humour here and there.

Development

This series was something I was thinking about for a while since I was in my early teens. But I haven't actually worked on it until around December 2023.

The way I implemented the SM64 animation movement to Roblox is via EgoMoose's Platformer character controller and Maximum_ADHD's funny 64-bit platformer. Along with extra minor features such as changeable faces, skybox color and more.

I was also originally planning to also remake Garry's mod on Roblox, but that got scrapped and I switched to using actual Garry's mod instead.

Maps also took a bit to do, since for the Roblox HQ map, I was modernising the classic HQ map for the series, while the house map is original.

Potential cancellation of the series

Recently with the way Roblox has been heading in terms of their updates and decisions. I've been feeling hella demotivated to continue this series.
Not to mention the risk of the tools such as RBX2Source breaking due to the forced dynamic heads and etc.

Along with taking too long to make a Roblox Bloopers video without it requiring custom maps, assets and more.
It's safe to say that this series may get discontinued in a year or two.

In terms of the series being community focus was also scrapped, due to it being a awful time to be a Roblox creator.

I've been also having a lot of mental breakdowns and panic attacks, as well as feeling hella depressed, all the way at the end of 2025 to present.

Roblox genuinely fucked me up in terms of me having a future, because without Roblox being a stepping stone, I don't have any talent that would help me have a job that I would truly care for.
And sure, I am semi good at drawing, video editing, animating and stuff. But lets face it, those skills alone won't actually get me a job.

If you take anything from this. If you think of an idea that has the potential to be successful, do it before its too late.
Roblox Bloopers was unfortunately too ambitious and too little, too late to actually succeed.
If I would have known that before, I would have started to work on Roblox Bloopers way earlier that I actually started, regardless how awful the quality would have been at the start.

It also does explain why Roblox Bloopers didn't caught on, unlike SM64 Bloopers. That genre alone died, because of how both the company and the platform of Roblox was evolving, unlike SM64 itself.
