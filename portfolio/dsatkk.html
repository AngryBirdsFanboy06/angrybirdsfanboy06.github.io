Basic Information

Dayshift At The Krusty Krab is a point and click style adventure game, created by Clock Studios (aka MasonYeetBoi).

Heavily inspired by Dayshift At Freddy's by DirectDoggo, where you work at the Krusty Krab, along with trying to impress the health inspector (or not), the game has multiple endings to earn.
