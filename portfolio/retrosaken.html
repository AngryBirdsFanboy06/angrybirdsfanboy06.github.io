Basic Information

RetroSaken is a modded version of a popular Roblox game called "Forsaken", where it replaces all of the characters and maps with known people, areas, inside jokes and more from the Retro Dev community.
Along with other original features and fixes.

My Involvement

Am an active contributor with making skins for survivors with the least skins and more, as well as being a tester.

The following skins that I've worked on are: RetroShorts, Retro Dev Wiki characters, DanTDM, Rthro Jimmy, Grandpa Charles, Mr. Retro and Virtual Insanity. (As well as a secret skin of Fanboy as Killer Homer)

I also animated one emotes called "My honest reaction to getting banned from Retro Dev". As well as suggesting a emote called "Cristiano's Worst Enemy". I was also involved with animating killer JunkClops, however my animations for it got replaced for good and was done by someone else. I was also involved with making the "Retrosaken Skin Character Spreadsheet"
