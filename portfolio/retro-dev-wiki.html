Basic Information

Retro Dev Wiki is a fan-created and community ran encyclopedia, that provides information about Retro Dev, a dev team that created both RETROBLOX and RetroStudio.

The wiki was founded by TheArtistTree on February 2021 along with more people contributing to the wiki site.

As of April 29th, 2026. There are 866 pages, 8,544 files and 45,090 edits.

My Involvement

I'm involved with a lot of stuff in regards to Retro Dev Wiki, such as moderating and updating the wiki site and the discord server, decision making in behind the scenes and more.

I was also involved with the Retro Dev Wiki clothing, which will soon go off sale due to Roblox being a piece of shit of a company.

On August 2021, I created my Fandom account to make a wiki article about myself. Keep in mind that NOBODY knew who I was.
However, I quickly became addicted with writing pages for Retro Dev Wiki, that I began documenting retro-dev.net, Retro Dev Discord bots and more.

On June 14, 2022. I got accepted as a moderator for Retro Dev Wiki after applying via a Google Form.
On September 12th 2022, I got promoted to an Admin, and later by default got promoted to Head Admin, along with all of the admins at the time.

Around April 14th, 2025, both me and the wiki team agreed that I would get promoted to an Head Admin+, in order to control the Retro Dev Wiki Discord Server via server roles and perms without TheArtistTree himself, mainly due to him being inactive with the Retro Dev Community, along with the wiki itself. This decision was a good thing, as it allowed us to promote Admins as Head Admins and more.
