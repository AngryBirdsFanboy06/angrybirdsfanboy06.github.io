Basic Information

On November 3rd 2024, I deleted both of my Twitter accounts due to them wanting to train everyones data for ai training.

I didnt want all of my stuff that ive posted on Twitter since 2019 to be misused like that, but at the same time, I wanted to make it accessable for anyone to view my old posts from my Twitter accounts. So before deleting my accounts, I downloaded everything on my accounts.

A day later, I uploaded everything (minus the personal info of course) and hosted it via Github.
