Basic Information

On November 3rd 2024, I deleted both of my Twitter accounts due to Twitter inc. wanting to train everyone's data for AI training.

I didn't want all of my stuff that I've posted on Twitter since 2019 to be misused like that, but at the same time, I wanted to make it accessible for anyone to view my old posts from my Twitter accounts. So before deleting my accounts, I downloaded everything on my accounts.

A day later, I uploaded everything (minus the personal info of course) and hosted it via Github.
