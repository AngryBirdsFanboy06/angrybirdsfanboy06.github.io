Information about TF2 SDK Games/SourceMods

Team Fortress 2 SDK Games also known as "TF2 SourceMods" are modified versions of Team Fortress 2, via the official TF2 SDK (or even via leaked code bases of older TF2 versions).

There are known TF2 SDK Game titles, such as Team Fortress 2 Classified (formerly known as TF2 Classic), Open Fortress, Pre-Fortress 2 and more.
On February 18th, 2025, Valve released the TF2 SDK to the public. A few days later, Team Fortress 2 Classified received a Steam store page and slowly more TF2 SDK Games followed through.
